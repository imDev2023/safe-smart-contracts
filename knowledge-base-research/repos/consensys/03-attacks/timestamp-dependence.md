Timestamp Dependence
====================

Be aware that the timestamp of the block can be manipulated by the miner, and all direct and indirect uses of the timestamp should be considered.

Note

See the [Recommendations](https://consensysdiligence.github.io/smart-contract-best-practices/development-recommendations/solidity-specific/timestamp-dependence/) section for design considerations related to Timestamp Dependence.

See [SWC-116](https://swcregistry.io/docs/SWC-116)
